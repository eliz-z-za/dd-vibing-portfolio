About Me

Who I Am

I'm a backend engineer with 5+ years of professional experience building scalable systems and APIs. I believe in clean code, thoughtful architecture, and systems that work reliably at scale.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Machine Learning while working full-time. My focus is on recommendation systems and how to apply ML in production environments.

What makes me unique is my balance between depth in backend development and breadth in creative pursuits. I'm equally comfortable discussing microservices architecture as I am talking about music production.

My Philosophy

  • Code Quality Over Speed: I'd rather ship something that works well tomorrow than something broken today.
  • Continuous Learning: Technology changes fast. I stay curious and constantly expand my skills.
  • Life Beyond Work: Balance is key. Creative pursuits make me a better engineer.
  • Build Real Things: I focus on solving actual problems, not theoretical exercises.
